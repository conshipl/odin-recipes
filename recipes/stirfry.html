Chicken Stir Fry

Description

This easy Chicken Stir Fry recipe is loaded with fresh veggies and the most delicious sauce made with honey, soy sauce, and toasted sesame oil! This healthy recipe takes 20 minutes to make and will wow your family with itâ€™s amazing flavor!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Broccoli
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Baby Corn
  • Baby Carrots
  • Garlic
  • Stir Fry Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook chicken in a skillet over medium-high heat (3-5 minutes)
  2. Remove chicken from skillet
  3. Cook vegetables until slightly crisp and tender
  4. Add garlic and cook until aromatic
  5. Readd chicken
  6. Add stir fry sauce and bring to a simmer
  7. Plate and enjoy!
